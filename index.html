ZERO

ZERO is a First Person Shooter game set in a zero gravity environtment where players can experience 360 movement where concept of up and down is irrelevant. In ZERO, player's objective is to loot as much item as they can and exfiltrate the area without dying in order to save their loot where they will lose all of their loot if the player died.
